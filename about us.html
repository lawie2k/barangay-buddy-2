About Us

BRIEF INTRODUCTION

Barangay Buddy is a digital platform designed to support barangays in transitioning to online services. By streamlining the processingxof documents and forms, we aim to make local government transactions easier, faster, and more convenient for both barangay officials and the people they serve.

MISSION
STATEMENT

Barangay Buddy is dedicated to helping barangays modernize their services by providing a user-friendly platform for online processing of documents and forms. We aim to make local government services faster, more accessible, and more efficient for every citizen.

Meet The Team

We’re a group of young innovators committed to making barangay services better through technology. Together, we design and build tools that help local communities work smarter and serve faster.
